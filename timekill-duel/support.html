Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to Play:

Starting the Game
- Launch the game and tap the START button to begin.

Game Mechanics
- Timer:
- Each level starts with a countdown timer (beginning at 1.0 second in early stages and decreasing at 10, 20, 30, and 50 kills).
- Tap the robot before the timer reaches 0.
- Kills:
- Each successful tap on the robot counts as a "kill."
- The kill count increases with each success.
- The robot can spawn anywhere on the screen, sometimes right on the edge, so you need to be quick.
- Bonus:
- When you kill the robot, the remaining time is added as bonus points for that level.
- Total Score:
- Calculated as total kills + total bonus achieved from all levels.
- Top Score: - If your Total Score exceeds your previous Top Score, it updates automatically.

Game Over
- If you fail to tap the robot before the timer reaches 0, the game ends.
- At game over, your kills and bonus points animate to display the final Total Score.
- The ad banner is displayed, with buttons to retry or exit.

Sound Options
- SOUND: ON / SOUND: OFF button toggles sound effects.
- When enabled, actions like shooting and collecting bonuses play sound effects.
- The setting is saved for future sessions.

Scoring Details
- Time: Levels start with a preset countdown that decreases over time.
- Bonus: Time remaining after a successful tap is converted into bonus points.
- Kills: Each tap on the robot increases the kill count.
- Total Score: Total Kills + Total Bonus points achieved.
- Top Score: The highest Total Score recorded across all play sessions.

Summary
- Quick Play: Ideal for short bursts of gameplay.
- Simple Mechanics: Tap the robot before time runs out.
- Increasing Challenge: Time limits decrease with progression.
- Rewards: Earn bonus points and track your high score.
- High Scores: Try to beat your best score with each game.
