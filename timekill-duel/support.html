Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to Play

Starting the Game: Launch the game and tap the START button to begin.

Game Mechanics

  • Timer: Starts at 1.0 second and decreases as you progress.
  • Kills: Tap the robot before the timer reaches 0.
  • Bonus: Remaining time converts into bonus points.
  • Total Score: Total Kills + Total Bonus points.
  • Top Score: The highest recorded score.

Game Over

The game ends if you fail to tap the robot before the timer reaches 0.

Sound Options

Toggle sound effects with the SOUND ON/OFF button. The setting is saved for future sessions.

Summary

  • Fast-paced gameplay, perfect for short sessions.
  • Increasing difficulty as time limits decrease.
  • Earn bonus points and track your high score.
